About Us!

We created this website to solve the problems of every book reader around the globe . It could be any student preparing for its competitive examination to a person who loves to read books . Our website “read o verse” gives users the opportunity to Rent a book rather than buying a new one . Generally, students who want to study a particular chapter from a particular book would have to buy a new book but our website gives them the opportunity to rent it with any one from the same city at very minimal cost. Another great feature of our website is that it provides every user its personal library (My Space) where a user can store all its Ebooks,Audiobooks at one place with a to-do list making feature and graphical analysis of all tasks(this feature is still under making phase..). Other than that, users can get information about lit-fests and book-fair going to take place in the city . In one tap the user provided information about all libraries near to them and users can also enjoy soothing music while reading , depending upon their moods.
